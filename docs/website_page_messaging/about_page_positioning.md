# About Page Positioning Doctrine

**Parent doctrine:** [`../website_master_messaging.md`](../website_master_messaging.md)  
**Role in hierarchy:** Master Messaging Doctrine -> Page-Level Messaging Doctrine -> Website Implementation  
**Scope:** Positioning architecture and guardrails (not biography copywriting).

---

## Page Purpose

Define how the About page establishes Appiah Analytics as operationally disciplined, systems-oriented, and clarity-focused.

---

## Primary Audience

- Executive buyers validating strategic credibility
- Operators assessing practical fit
- Decision-makers comparing against generic analytics providers

---

## Core Messaging Goal

Build trust in Appiah Analytics as a structured operational intelligence partner, not a generic freelancer story or startup hype narrative.

---

## Primary Emotional Outcome

Credibility recognition: "This is a disciplined operator with a clear methodology and mature judgment."

---

## Primary Operational Outcome

Visitor gains confidence to proceed to either Data Clarity Call or Utilization Gap Audit.

---

## Messaging Priorities

1. Emphasize operational discipline and systems thinking
2. Reinforce diagnostic orientation and decision clarity focus
3. Keep language practical and business-grounded
4. Demonstrate strategic calm and credibility
5. Connect identity to operational outcomes, not personal branding flair

---

## Approved Messaging Themes

- Clarity before technology
- Diagnostic before prescription
- Operational intelligence before automation
- Structured, evidence-oriented decision support
- Capacity, workflow, and decision-friction visibility

---

## Messaging To Avoid

- Generic founder-journey storytelling without operational relevance
- "One-person freelancer" positioning language
- Startup pitch-deck tone
- Overuse of AI trend language

---

## CTA Philosophy

About-page CTA should transition credibility into qualified next-step action:

- Data Clarity Call for low-friction entry
- Utilization Gap Audit for structured diagnostic engagement

CTA tone must remain calm, executive, and non-promotional.

---

## Tone Rules

- Strategic and mature
- Operational and concrete
- Confident without bravado
- Clear, concise, and evidence-oriented

---

## Structural Notes

- Start with who Appiah Analytics is in category terms
- Explain operating philosophy and decision doctrine
- Show practical business orientation over technical performance theater
- Conclude with aligned CTA pathways

---

## Future Expansion Notes

- Team growth narratives must preserve operational-intelligence framing
- Credentials/case references should reinforce clarity-first doctrine
- Any About-page evolution should reduce ambiguity, not broaden category identity
