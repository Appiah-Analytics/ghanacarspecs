# Home Page Messaging Doctrine

**Parent doctrine:** [`../website_master_messaging.md`](../website_master_messaging.md)  
**Role in hierarchy:** Master Messaging Doctrine -> Page-Level Messaging Doctrine -> Website Implementation  
**Scope:** Governance guidance only (not production copy).

---

## Page Purpose

Define how the homepage establishes Appiah Analytics as a diagnostic operational intelligence and decision-clarity firm, while directing qualified visitors toward a Data Clarity Call.

---

## Primary Audience

- Agency owners and leadership teams
- Operations and delivery leaders
- Decision-makers experiencing utilization drag, workflow fragmentation, or recurring decision bottlenecks

---

## Core Messaging Goal

Create immediate category clarity: Appiah Analytics is not a dashboard/BI vendor, but a clarity-first diagnostic partner focused on operational leakage and decision quality.

---

## Primary Emotional Outcome

Calm confidence: "Someone understands our operational friction and can help us see it clearly."

---

## Primary Operational Outcome

A qualified visitor chooses the front-door CTA and books a Data Clarity Call.

---

## Messaging Priorities

1. Lead with clarity-first positioning
2. Frame business friction before technical discussion
3. Emphasize utilization leakage, fragmented workflows, and decision bottlenecks
4. Keep all messaging grounded, executive, and diagnostic
5. Route toward a clarity conversation, not a capability deep-dive

---

## Approved Messaging Themes

- Clarity before technology
- Diagnostic before prescription
- Operational intelligence before automation
- Capacity leakage visibility
- Better operational decisions over more reporting artifacts

---

## Messaging To Avoid

- Generic BI/dashboard language
- Feature-heavy technical explanations on first screen
- SaaS/platform positioning
- Hype-heavy AI framing
- "Total transformation" promises

---

## CTA Philosophy

Primary CTA intent: drive qualified Data Clarity Calls.

Preferred CTA direction:

- "Book your free 15-minute Data Clarity Call"
- "No obligation. No sales pressure. Just clarity."

CTA guardrail: avoid vague generic CTAs that do not reinforce diagnostic positioning.

---

## Tone Rules

- Executive and operational
- Disciplined and specific
- Calm, credible, and non-hype
- Business-outcome oriented

---

## Structural Notes

- Hero should establish category identity quickly
- Mid-page sections should reinforce operational problems and diagnostic lens
- CTA sections should remain tightly aligned with low-pressure clarity conversation
- Avoid technical overload; reserve methods/detail for deeper pages

---

## Future Expansion Notes

- Any new homepage modules must inherit master doctrine language
- Additional sections should deepen operational clarity, not broaden into service sprawl
- New CTA variants must still prioritize qualified clarity conversations
